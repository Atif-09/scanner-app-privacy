Information Collection and Use

While the Docs Scanner app itself does not collect any personal information or request permissions directly, it uses Google ML Kit Document Scanner, which requires access to your device's camera and gallery.

  • Camera Access: The SDK uses your camera to detect and scan physical documents. No images or data are stored or transmitted by the Docs Scanner app.
  • Gallery Access: If you choose to select an existing document from your gallery, the SDK requires access to your gallery. The app does not store or share this data.

All document processing is done locally on your device. The Docs Scanner app does not transmit, collect, or store any document data outside of the app.
